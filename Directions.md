# Forza Horizon 6 Top Gear Special
### Timeline and Driving Instructions

> All letters can be found in the Letters folder.

## Episode 1 : Southern Shimanoyama and Nangan

>Read Ep 1 Letter 1.

1. Beginning at the coliseum, complete the Stadium Backroad speed trap just outside the coliseum, 3 tries each.

2. Take the road south, and complete the Fuji Shibazakura speed zone, 3 tries each.

3. Continue south, passing through the Kawazu Nanadaru loop bridge before turning right at the next paved road intersection, towards the 3 hairpins. Stay straight through the large triangle of roads before turning right down the road which takes you south through the middle of the forest. Complete the Hakone Turns speed zone, 3 tries each.

4. Turn left down the dirt road and complete the Nangan Turn speed trap, 3 tries each.

>Read Ep 1 Letter 2.

5. Turn right when you reach the road, go complete the Irokawa Launch danger sign, 1 try each.

6. Go to the Irokawa Quarter Mile drag meet, and compete in a best of 5 championship.

## Episode 2: Tokyo City and Southern Ito

>Read Ep 2 Letter 1.

1. From the drag meet, take the seaside road north towards tokyo. Continue north until you can turn right onto the road leading to Shibuya Crossing spped trap. Complete the event, 3 tries each.

2. Continue east until the road curves south-east. Complete the Akihabara Straight speed trap, 3 tries each.

3. Continue down the road south-east towards the Daikoku Parking Area, and complete the speed trap, 3 tries each.

4. Get on the highway and complete the Ocean Highway speed zone, 3 tries each.

5. OPTIONAL: Get off the highway and complete the Tokyo City Docks drift zone. 1 try each.

>Read Episode 2 Letter 2.

6. Go to and complete the Tanbo Launch danger sign, 1 try each.

7. Go to the Sekibe Time Attack and complete it, 5 tries each.
