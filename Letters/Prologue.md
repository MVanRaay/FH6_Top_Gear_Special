# Forza Horizon 6 Top Gear Special
### Prologue

Hello! And welcome, to a very special edition of Top Gear&reg;. This time around, you and as many of your buddies as you'd like will be taking a trip around the entirety of Japan in Forza Horizon 6&reg;! Travelling (probably, I haven't counted) 100km+ and completing over 30 challenges, this will be a long expedition, so it's split into multiple episodes. While travelling across Japan, you will acquire 4 vehicles, spanning different histories and cultures. Your adventure begins at the coliseum all the way at the far west of the map, before passing through every region in the game, apart from Legend Island, and ending at the Horizon Festival site.

>ATTENTION: For the best experience, make sure to follow the instructions in the Setup.md file.
